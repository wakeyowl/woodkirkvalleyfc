Defending Principles

• Maintain shape as a defensive unit to prevent penetrating passes • Defensive compactness • Shift quickly as a unit to screen the penetrating pass (ball side) • Defenders to attempt to win the ball back as soon as possible and apply pressure • Decisions and communication to determine roles of defenders - who presses, who supports, who provides cover and balance? • Communication between defenders to develop awareness of movement and effectively defend • Read play and cues from attackers (head down, direction of passes) to be in a position to press and regain the ball • Supporting defenders to get in a position to provide cover for teammate • Stop opposition playing forward

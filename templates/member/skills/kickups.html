Kick-Ups

  • Singles kick and catch
  • Use both feet
  • Increase to two kicks then catch so you can do it consistently
  • Keep increasing as your confidence grows
  • Try bounce kickups - 1 bounce / 1 kick / 1 bounce ...
  • Use different body parts
Coaches Tips !


  • Lean slightly backwards
  • Toes pointing upwards
  • Eyes always on the ball
  • Weight of touch - Keep ball height just above your knee
  • Don't hit it too hard
  • Don't Rush

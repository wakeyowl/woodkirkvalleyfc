Club Registration

As a adult you need to register with Woodkirk Valley FC as a club member, you will then either register as a individual player or as the parent of a player or players.

The player registration details will be submitted to the FA whole game system so all players are officially registered with the league.
For the club secretary to register players on the whole game system you will now be required to submit a player photo.
Players will be unable to play for the club until this process is completed.
